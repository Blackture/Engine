Contact And Chat Rules

Connect with the Discord community to share ideas and collaborate, fostering a spirit of mutual assistance prior to reaching out for individual support. Please report bugs, submit ideas or request features via Discord or the corresponding form.

By participating or reaching out, you agree to:

  • Use Discord or the Corresponding Form: Please use Discord prior reaching out using the support form. Use the form that corresponds to why you're reaching out and contact only once per subject (no duplicates).
  • Promote Respectful Communication: Foster a culture of respectful interaction and communication.
  • Constructive Feedback: Feedback will only be accepted if it's helpful, constructive, or thoughtful, where the or is non-exclusive.
  • Uphold Positive Tone: Maintain a positive and encouraging tone in all interactions.
  • Embrace Supportive Language: Use language that uplifts and supports others in your conversations.
  • Value Differences: Embrace diverse viewpoints and engage in open discussions.
  • Highlight Kindness: Show kindness and empathy in all interactions.
  • Advocate for Politeness: Use polite and courteous language in your communication.
  • Share Your Ideas, If You Want: Share your thoughts and ideas openly, if you want to.
  • Respect Boundaries: Be mindful of personal boundaries and respect others' opinions.
  • Only send content (name, email, message), that:
    • contains only expressions or similar, that comply with applicable national, local or international law or regulation
    • complies only to the truth
    • is only praises, compliments, or supports others
  • Refrain from sending any type of ad
  • Refrain sending anything that is for the purpose exploiting, harming or attempting to exploit or harm minors in any way
  • Refrain sending anything that is discriminating anything, any body, any group, any culture, ...
  • Refrain sending anything that is exploiting any of the vulnerabilities of a specific group of persons based on their age, social, physical or mental characteristics, in order to materially distort the behavior of a person pertaining to that group in a manner that causes or is likely to cause that person or another person physical or psychological harm
  • Refrain sending stereotypes about anybody, any culture, ...
